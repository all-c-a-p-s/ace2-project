61 variables which is the variables needed as inputs (same as in initial_conditions) + some other diagnostic variables

Precipitation is stored outputted as `PRATEsfc`: `{'units': 'kg/m**2/s', 'long_name': 'Surface precipitation rate'}` (i.e. kg of rainfall per metre squared of surface per second, over the last 6 hour window).

About 16MB and < 5 secs per set of predictions
